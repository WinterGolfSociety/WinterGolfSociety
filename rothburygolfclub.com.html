Welcome to Rothbury Golf Club

Welcome to Rothbury Golf Club

Rothbury Golf Course is a picturesque, 18-hole course that nestles in the heart of Northumberland’s Coquet Valley and is within easy walking distance of the historic village of Rothbury.

The course, adjoining Northumberland National Park, benefits from spectacular views of the Coquet Valley and the distant Cheviot Hills, and is bounded by the Simonside Hills, the village itself, the Cragside estate and the meandering River Coquet.

For Open Competitions; Members and Visitors please use the following link https://www.rothburygolfclub.com/

Visitors are always welcome and details of our individual green fees and golf society packages can be found in the Visitor section.

As part of the club’s commitment to Equality and Diversity we are proud to say we are now signatories to the R&A Women in Golf Charter, encouraging more women and girls to play and participate in all aspects of golf club life.

The Clubhouse, hosting Yvonne’s Cakes & Catering, Coffee Shop Café

The bar and restaurant at our clubhouse afford spectacular views of the Coquet Valley and Cheviot Hills.

Please check Yvonnes Cakes & Catering Coffe Shop Cafe facebook page for opening times.

Closed: Mondays & Thursdays

Yvonne’s Café offers a wide range of delicious artisan cakes, light meals and snacks, as well as specialty tees, coffees and milkshakes to eat in or take away . Our catering team put their years of experience into play to make sure all of our visitors enjoy excellent, home-cooked fare.

With extensive seating in the restaurant and bar, a large outside deck for sunny days and ample parking, the clubhouse is ideal for parties and celebrations.

Thank you for visiting our website, if you have any questions about the club, course, events or any other matter please don’t hesitate to contact us.

Visiting Parties

The person making the reservation agrees to take responsibility for the actions of the group:

  • Ensuring everyone in the group is in good health before starting.
  • Booking the tee time, ensuring all players' names are reserved, and making any changes to the booking.
  • Checking in at the Clubhouse prior to teeing off
  • Ensure the group tees off at their reserved time. Not before or after.
  • Ensuring that the group abides by the Clubs rules.
